Common Questions

Everything you
want to know.

How long does a modular kitchen take from start to handover?

From design approval to final handover, our standard timeline is 38–45 days — covering 3D design (5 days), production (20–25 days), and installation (5–7 days). Rush delivery is available, with turnarounds as fast as 28 days for a premium.

What does a modular kitchen typically cost?

A standard 10–12 ft L-shaped kitchen starts at ₹1.8L with membrane finish and basic hardware. Premium acrylic or PU finishes with Hettich or Blum hardware range from ₹3.5L–₹7L. We provide a fully itemised quote with zero hidden charges before any commitment.

What warranty comes with a KraftKitchen?

10-year structural warranty on all cabinets and carcasses. 5-year warranty on hardware (hinges, drawer channels, lift systems). After-sales service within 48 hours in Delhi NCR and 72 hours in Mumbai. We stand behind everything we build.

Can you retrofit an existing or rental kitchen?

Absolutely. We specialise in both full installations and retrofit solutions. For rental properties we offer semi-permanent modular systems designed to be relocated when you move. We also handle partial upgrades — countertop replacement, new shutters, or hardware swaps.

Is production in-house or outsourced to vendors?

100% in-house. Every cabinet is manufactured at our CNC-equipped factory in Delhi — we never outsource to third parties. This means we control quality at every single stage, from raw board to finished unit. No surprises, no quality shortcuts.

Which hardware brands do you use?

We are authorised dealers for Hettich, Häfele, and Blum — three of Europe's most respected kitchen hardware brands. All drawer systems, hinges, and lift mechanisms carry manufacturer guarantees. We also offer Ebco for value-conscious projects without compromising durability.

Do I see the 3D design before making any payment?

Yes — always. We provide a free photorealistic 3D render before any payment is required. You'll see your exact kitchen — with your chosen finish, layout, lighting, and handles — before a single unit goes into production. Unlimited revisions included.

Which cities do you currently serve?

Full service across Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad (complete Delhi NCR) and Mumbai, Thane, and Navi Mumbai. Expanding to Pune and Bengaluru soon. Large residential developments outside these zones can be accommodated on request.
